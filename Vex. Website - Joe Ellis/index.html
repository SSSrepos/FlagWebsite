Welcome to the site about the best thing since sliced bread - Flags!

This website is the go-to place for your flag needs, whether it's designing a flag or just reading about it! 😄

And remember the five basic rules of designing flags:

  1. Keep it simple
  2. Use meaningful symbolism
  3. Use two or three basic colours
  4. No lettering or seals
  5. Be distinctive or related to another flag (without copying it)
